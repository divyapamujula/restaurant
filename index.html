Discover Something Healthy & Delicious

In 2005, Veda began a revolution in the fast food industry launching a unique market niche in North America. Our founder's vision was to create the first and only healthy Indian fast food takeout concept. Our goal was to provide a healthy and delicious meal experience for busy people looking to have their food brought to them when and where they want it. After more than 13 years and over 1 million baked samosas later, Veda has expanded from takeout roots, to focusing on home and office delivery, and now private, campus, and corporate catering. Pop in to one of our two downtown Toronto locations, or even better, let us bring the food to you!
